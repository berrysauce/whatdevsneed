What is the difference between Free, Freemium, and Premium?
These are the different pricing models of the tools.
Free: Completely free, without paid plans
Freemium: Partly free, with basic free and paid plans
Paid: Not free, no free plans

I added my tool. What now?
You may or may not get an email about your listing. To make sure it was added, just search for it 1-2 days after submitting it. I do not verify each submission.

My tool wasn't added. Why?
Sometimes, the tool just doesn't fit or it's straight up not a developer tool. If your submission wasn't added, do not re-submit it.
